Kazakhstan

Located in Central Asia, Kazakhstan, often abbreviated as KZ, is a republic known for its rich nomadic history, stunning landscapes, and the fusion of modern and traditional architecture. The largest landlocked country in the world is organized into 14 regions, each offering a unique blend of past and present.

Georgia

Georgia's nature is renowned for its geographical and biological diversity. In addition to the mountains, it has all types of forests and thousands of local species of flora and fauna. Georgia's nature is diverse in animals, including, caucasian red deer, roe deer, wild boar, rabbits, and squirrels.

Japan

The best time to visit Japan is during spring (March to May) and fall (September to November). This is when Japan is at its most vibrant, with delicate cherry blossom or bright red leaves adding contrast to the scenery. Remember, it can also be very crowded at this time.
